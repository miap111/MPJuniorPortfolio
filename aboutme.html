All about Mia Pinto.

My Bio:

I'm a web developer and aspiring creative technologist with a passion for building interactive, visually engaging digital experiences. I’m certified in HTML and have completed multiple web projects, competed in web design contests, including earning 3rd place in the Citywide Graphic Arts Competition, participated in a hackathon, and consistently apply my Python skills in class. I love combining coding skills with design to create projects that are both functional and visually compelling.

Beyond programming, you can find me on the bleachers cheering on the boys' basketball team, in the library helping people find their next perfect read, or in the Culinary Arts room assisting my peers. I serve as Editor for the 11th-grade creative writing class, mentoring writers and helping curate engaging content which is eventually published in The Edison Spark.

Fun Fact:

My dream job title is Creative Director! To make this possible I want to pursue a Business degree with a Marketing/Management major and a Graphic Arts minor. I'm currently looking at FIT and Baruch College!
